Our Story

Jautr is led by Art T., a Sacramento‑area software engineer who has spent a decade building high‑reliability systems for healthcare, civic, and small‑business clients. Art carries two convictions into every engagement: public‑impact work deserves first‑class tech, and lean teams can out‑deliver large vendors when they collapse strategy and implementation into the same set of hands.

What We Do

We transform confusing online forms into streamlined, mobile‑friendly intakes. Replace manual data shuffles with secure automated pipelines. Integrate disconnected payment flows. Provide live dashboards instead of spreadsheet exports. And relieve staff of repetitive tasks through safe, private AI that handles the drudgery.

Why Choose Jautr

Single‑threaded ownership means strategy, code, testing, and rollout live with one accountable engineer. We measure success through wait‑time reduction and error rates, deliver prototypes in days without shortcuts, and design for clarity first. Our engagements scale from two-week fixes to multi-phase modernization roadmaps.

The Jautr Method

A proven approach that delivers value at every stage

  • Discovery & Evidence

    We sit with frontline users, observe the clicks, and quantify the delay. Every automation starts with hard numbers.

  • Blueprint & Prototype

    Within days you see a working slice—no 80‑page deck, just an interactive demo tied to your data.

  • Agile Implementation

    Weekly checkpoints keep scope honest. We deploy incrementally so value arrives while we're still talking about it.

  • Human-Centered Validation

    Usability sessions with actual constituents or members ensure the new flow feels intuitive, inclusive, and accessible.

  • Secure Launch & Knowledge Transfer

    Infrastructure hardened, staff trained, documentation delivered. Ownership stays with you.

  • Sustain & Iterate

    Light‑touch support agreements keep the system patched, measured, and improved as policy or volume shifts.

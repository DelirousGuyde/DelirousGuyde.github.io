About Jautr

Purpose‑built digital craftsmanship for public‑facing teams

Our Story

Jautr is led by Art T., a Sacramento‑area software engineer who has spent a decade building high‑reliability systems for healthcare, civic, and small‑business clients. Art carries two convictions into every engagement:

  1. Public‑impact work deserves first‑class tech.
    DMV lines, outdated membership portals, and opaque grant applications are not inevitable; they are solvable design problems.
  2. Lean teams can out‑deliver large vendors when they collapse strategy and implementation into the same set of hands.
    As a solo founder Art codes the solution he imagines—no layers of account management separating insight from execution.

These beliefs took shape when Art spotted a scheduling and payment mix‑up at a local ranch while buying bulk beef. A few lines of Python and a lightweight AI model would have prevented hours of manual reconciliation. That real‑world friction became the template: observe a concrete pain point, build a rapid fix, prove the ROI, expand responsibly. Jautr now applies that loop to organizations whose processes touch thousands of lives instead of dozens.

The Road to Partnership

  1. Intro Call (15 min) – share the bottleneck; we confirm fit.
  2. Workflow Leak Audit (fixed cost) – onsite or virtual, delivers a lo‑fi process map, quantified pain points, and an automation feasibility score.
  3. Statement of Work – clear timeline, deliverables, and acceptance criteria.
  4. Iterative Delivery – you preview and approve each slice; no black‑box surprises.
  5. Launch & Reflection – we compare before/after metrics and capture lessons for Phase Two if desired.
